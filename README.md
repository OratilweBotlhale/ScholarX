# ScholarX
## Purpose of ScholarX:
ScholarX is a website for a financial support bursary that funds learners that are interested in pursuing in their dreams in the STEM industries. We not only provide financial support, but we have a team of professionals that are dedicated to guiding students through the day-to-day life of university, and to ultimately finding valuable jobs in their chosen fields of study. 

## Mission of ScholarX: 
Our mission is to tackle and fight the crisis that South Africa is currently facing, "(Youth Unemployment)" by making sure that the youth of South Africa have the necessary funding to study in SCIENCE, TECHNOLOGY, ENGINEERING, AND MATHEMATICS which are major fields that are in demand. With ScholarX, students can use our well-established website to apply for their preferred study of choice, and we will evaluate and choose students with pleasing marks. The overall mission of ScholarX is to identify, support, and empower exceptional students who demonstrate both academic potential and a commitment to making a positive impact in their communities

## Intended Audience
Students 
•	Bursary will be awarded for studies within the following fields that are in STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics) it can be a bachelor’s degree or a Postgraduate degree. 
•	Requires a platform to check bursary requirements and apply their application online.
•	An update on the application status of a student and information about any other available bursary programs available.
University Administrators
•	The University needs to approve and manage bursary applications of students securely. 
•	The University needs to validate students’ academic performance and students’ information.
Sponsors and funding organizations 
•	The institutions and companies that need to fund and provide bursaries to students. 
•	A platform to post opportunities and view students who qualify for the bursary.
