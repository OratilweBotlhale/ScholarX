About ScholarX

ScholarX is a financial support bursary that funds learners that are interested in pursuing in their dreams in the STEM industries. We not only provide financial support, but we have a team of professionals that are dedicated to guiding students through the day-to-day life of university, and to ultimately finding valuable jobs in their chosen fields of study.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between talented students and financial opportunities, ensuring that no learner is left behind due to lack of resources.

Our Vision

To become Africa’s leading digital bursary and scholarship application platform.

Our Core Values

These principles guide everything we do and shape the ScholarX experience.

Excellence

We strive for the highest standards in education and support.

Accessibility
